Below are some common types of cat breeds, check it out!

1. Ragdoll Cat

Ragdoll cat.The most suitable word to describe ragdoll cat is docile.It doesn’t means that they are inactive.Ragdoll cat are very sociable and love to stay close with owners or even the house guests. They likes to join in family activities and collapse into the arm of anyone who hold them. As their long fur is prone to tangles, they need to be brushed and combed often.

Fun Fact: Ragdolls love running water and will often play in the shower or sink.

Life Span:12-17 years

Length: 17-21 inches,not including tail

Weight: 10-20 pounds


2. Persian Cat

Persian cat.Dignity and gentleness is synonymous with Persian cat.Persian cat tends to be relaxed and easy going, they also command an air of royalty. They are happy to be combed and petted by children, but are unlikely to join in boisterous games with them.Persian cat are very laid back and much less disruptive and curios than other breeds.

Fact: Persian cats are one of the most commonly featured breeds in movies like James Bond and Austin Powers, and are a Hollywood icon!

Life Span：10-15 years

Length: 14-18 inches, not including tail

Weight: 7-12 pounds


3. American Short Hair Cat

American Short Hair. The American Shorthair cat reflects the feline look of their hunting ancestors, the short, lustrous coat and the sweet, open expression. This breed is known for its longevity, robust health, friendly personality and patience with dogs and other pets. They are self-sufficient and easygoing. American shorthairs don't need a lot of attention to be happy. Because of this, they are a great choice of cat for a single person who works all day.

Fun Fact : They love to eat rodents and insects. If your American shorthair is allowed to roam outside, you will be delighted to discover frequent gifts of furry prey on your doorstep. An ambitious kitty may even find rodents lurking in your basement or garage.

Life Span:15-20 years

Length: 12-15 inches

Weight: 7-12 pounds


4. Burmese Cat

Burmese cat. A good climber and jumper, the Burmese loves being adored by her parent but may not get along easily with other cat breeds. Burmese cats are often referred to as "bricks wrapped in silk". When you pick her up, she feels considerably heavier than she appears. Burmese cats are known for their distinctive meow, which sounds like a low rumble. They will often use this to get your attention when they are hungry or are seeking affection.

Fact: The term “curious as a cat” could be coined for Burmese cats. They are extremely inquisitive and are always into something. Thy need to be constantly entertained so make sure you have enough time to devote to this playful breed!

Life Span: 10-16 years

Length: 15-18 inches

Weight: 8-12 pounds
