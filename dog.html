Below are some common types of dog breeds, check it out!

1. Toy Poodle

Poodle. Named for their love of water. Poodles are one of the world’s oldest dog breeds. Poodles do not shed as much as other breeds and do not have the typical “dog smell” so they are the ideal pet for those who prefer a “tidy” dog breed. Poodles love to travel and make great companion dogs for those who love to take long road trips or even fly.

Fact: Toy poodles and Poodles are in fact the same breed of dog, just selectively bred to reach a miniature size!

Life Span: 10-18 years

Height: From 10 to over 22 inches tall at the shoulder

Weight: 6-70 pounds


2. Golden Retriever

Golden Retriever. The Golden Retriever is one of the most popular dog breeds in the United States. Golden Retrievers make fantastic family pets,it do extremely well in family settings with or without young children, but they are also versatile sporting and working dogs.

Fact: Golden Retrievers come in various shades of gold which usually darken as they grow.

Life Span: 10 to 12 years

Height: 21 to 24 inches tall at the shoulder

Weight: 55 to 75 pounds


3. Maltese

Maltese. A breed having enjoyed its royal status as canine royalty for centuries. Maltese are affectionate toy dogs weighing less than seven pounds, covered by a long and white coat.

Fact: The maltese looks a lot like Saruman XD. Maltese is almost as old as time itself.While that is a bit of an exaggeration, the history of the Maltese likely dates back to the Phoenicians who brought the dog to Malta.

Life Span: 12 to 15 years

Height: 8 to 10 inches tall at the shoulder

Weight: Up to 7 pounds
